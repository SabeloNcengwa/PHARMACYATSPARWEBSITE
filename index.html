Your health, handled with care.

Trusted pharmacy services, clinic support, and everyday wellness—right here in your community.

Welcome to Pharmacy at SPAR Mega City, where everyday healthcare is fast, safe, and easy to access. Stop by our in-store clinic for friendly health consultations and basic screenings, and get clear guidance on how to manage common conditions. Our dispensary supports both acute and chronic medication needs, ensuring prescriptions are dispensed accurately and with professional advice. If you’re registered for CCMDD, you can collect your chronic medicine from our convenient pickup point to help reduce queues and waiting time. In the front shop, we also offer a wide range of over-the-counter essentials, personal care products, wellness items, and supplements—so you can ask questions and choose with confidence from a qualified team. We respect your privacy and focus on helping you feel well, informed, and supported.

Who We Are

About Pharmacy at Spar Mega City

Pharmacy at Spar Mega City is committed to delivering trusted, accessible, and high-quality healthcare services to our community. As we relaunch, we continue our journey with a renewed focus on excellence, innovation, and patient-centered care.

We are more than a pharmacy — we are a healthcare partner. Our goal is to provide accurate medication, professional advice, and a welcoming environment where every customer feels valued and supported.

👁️

Our Vision

To be the most trusted community pharmacy, known for exceptional service, professional integrity, and a positive impact on the health and wellbeing of every customer we serve.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide safe, affordable, and high-quality pharmaceutical care through expert knowledge, modern services, and a customer-first approach that promotes healthier lives.

👥

Our Customer Base

We proudly serve a diverse community, including families, senior citizens, working professionals, students, and individuals managing chronic conditions. Our pharmacy caters to both walk-in customers and repeat patients who rely on us for consistent and dependable healthcare support.

⚕️

Our Team

Our pharmacy is staffed by qualified and experienced professionals, including registered pharmacists, pharmacy assistants, and support staff. Every team member is trained to deliver friendly service, accurate dispensing, and confidential healthcare advice.
